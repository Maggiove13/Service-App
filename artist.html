Our Creative Team

Meet the talented artists behind our amazing 3D creations.

Paul Astoria

Architectural Visualization Consultant

His job is to make projects look so stunning that clients forget these buildings are just fancy drawings. Perfect for selling dreams - just be careful not to fall head over heels for a blueprint that'll never see the light of day!

Toby Queen

3D Modeler and Digital Chaos Master

His specialty is creating models so complex that they sometimes wonder if they're designing a building or a 1,000-piece puzzle. Perfect for wowing clients, but make sure you've got a PhD in "How the Heck Does This Thing Work?" manual!

Chany Still

Lighting Specialist and video maker

Her job is to ensure every building shines brighter than a Hollywood celebrity... sometimes forgetting this isn't a Christmas light competition. Perfect for illuminating your ideas, but watch out for those unexpected lens flares!

Zara Spark

Interior Designer with a sparkling touch

SHer job is to make every space cozy and vibrant, but she often turns offices into nightclubs. Perfect for grand openings, but we can't guarantee you'll get any work done! When she's not transforming workspaces, she's creating hilarious design videos that could make HGTV look like a snooze fest - expect unexpected dance moves, design puns, and more sass than your average interior design tutorial.

Max Thunder

Resilient Structures Expert

His motto is "stronger than thunder", but you'll often find him huddled under an umbrella in the office. Perfect for designing buildings that can withstand hurricanes, though he occasionally forgets minor details... like, oh, windows! Rumor has it he once designed a structure so robust that even Mother Nature would need a permission slip to approach it.
